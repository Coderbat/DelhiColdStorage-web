Welcome to Delhi Cold Storage

Delhi Cold Storage Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 17 June 1946. We are glad to introduce ourselves as leaders in processing frozen fruits and vegetables. Specializing in developing, growing, and processing all fruits and vegetables, milk products, at blast freezing, food and many more. Our decade’s long experience and finest infrastructure have aided us to earn recognition as one of the leaders in this field which in turn enables us to work with extremely reputed big names in the various industries. Our success is an outcome of strategic focus & constant investing in state-of-the-art infrastructure & a team of experienced experts, that deliver utmost hygiene, safety & unmatchable services. We are engaged in continuous improvement of our services to enhance the quality and cost competitiveness to build value for our customers. It is our immense pleasure to say that we are client client-centric organization. We are committed to our clients and willing to deliver a flexible, customer-orientated approach to meet the individual needs of our business. We endeavor to team up with small and large companies alike to provide healthy, superior, and affordable services to meet the demands of the market.

Our Services

Deep Freezer

The Deep Freezer use State of Art of insulations panels made of Polyurethane panels of 120 mm thickness having metal skins on both the sides. Offer warehouse facilities of products at - 18° centigrade . Refrigeration equipment imported from Bitzer, Germany and Copeland, USA. Uses Enviroment friendly refrigrant Gas R-404 A. Power Backup.

Refrigerated Warehousing

Offers warehousing facilities of products at 0° - 2° Centigrade . Specialized Ethylene free chambers for imported and Indian fruits. Power Back Up. Convenient operating hours from 6.00 AM to 10.00 PM.

Carrying & Forwarding Agents

Offers services for delivery of milk and frozen foods products to the retailers and end consumers from the producer, importers and whole sellers in Delhi & NCR. These services provided are:Storage of frozen and agri products. Collection of orders from retailers. Delivery of products. Invoicing of products delivered to the retailers. Collection of payment. MIS. Statutory/Sales Tax compliances.
